# UNIX/32V (Reiser & London, Bell Labs, 1979)

The port of V7 to the DEC VAX-11/780.  THE hinge point where AT&T
and Berkeley Unix diverge.  Above 32V there is one Unix; below it
there are two lineages (AT&T → System III → SVR* → Solaris ->
your heirloom-toolchest-darwin's bin/; Berkeley → 1-4BSD →
2.11BSD → BSDs → your heirloom-toolchest-darwin's ucb/).

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Documentation/Manuals/UNIX_32V_Version_1.0/`
  (32V manual pages)

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Every user of `/opt/heirloom/ucb/ls` versus `/opt/heirloom/bin/ls`
is standing on the fault line 32V opened.  Studying 32V source
explains, in source form, why heirloom-toolchest-darwin ships two
personality variants of the same commands.

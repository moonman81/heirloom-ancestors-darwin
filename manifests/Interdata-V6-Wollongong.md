# V6 Interdata 7/32 port (Wollongong, 1977)

Australian University of Wollongong's port of V6 to the Interdata 7/32
(a 32-bit minicomputer). Historically important as one of the
FIRST-ever ports of Unix to non-PDP-11 hardware — pre-32V by two years.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Other/Interdata/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Pioneering Unix port. Illustrates that portability preceded 32V's
Vax port (which is often incorrectly credited as the first Unix
port off PDP-11).

# UNSW AUSAM series (Sydney, 1978-1985)

Australian University of New South Wales' extended Unix distribution.
22 numbered site tapes on TUHS containing additions to V6 and V7:
early networking, sfio predecessors, and community tools.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UNSW/` (22 numbered
  tapes: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 106, 107, 108, 110, ...)

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Historically foundational for the Australian Unix community;
particular relevance for `heirloom-vi-darwin` and for AUUGN
newsletter cross-references.

# Taylor UUCP (Ian Lance Taylor, 1990s)

Ian Taylor's free reimplementation of UUCP.  Historically the primary
open-source UUCP implementation; ships with most late-1990s Unix
distributions.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/TaylorUUCP/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Heirloom does NOT currently ship UUCP.  Taylor UUCP is a plausible
future addition to the heirloom-*-darwin family (potentially as
`heirloom-uucp-darwin`) for users who want a full historical Unix
mail/news stack.

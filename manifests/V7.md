# V7 (Version 7 Unix, Bell Labs, 1979)

The direct AT&T ancestor of every SVR4/POSIX Unix.  Where the Bourne
shell was born; where troff was refined; where most of the toolchest
commands acquired their canonical form.

## Content summary

Spencer's tape (v7.tar.gz, 3.4 MB compressed) contains:
- `usr/src/cmd/` — canonical AT&T commands (~140 utilities)
  including the direct ancestors of every heirloom-toolchest tool
- `usr/src/cmd/sh/` — Stephen Bourne's original /bin/sh (direct
  ancestor of heirloom-sh-darwin's src/sh/)
- `usr/src/cmd/awk/` — later added (1977-79 lineage)
- `usr/src/cmd/troff/`, `nroff/`, `tbl/`, `eqn/` — the doctools
  pipeline in its 1979 form
- `usr/src/sys/` — the V7 kernel

# em — Editor for Mortals (George Coulouris, Queen Mary College, 1976)

The Queen Mary College screen-oriented editor that Bill Joy brought
back to Berkeley and used as the seed for `ex` → `vi`.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Em_Editor/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Direct ancestor of `heirloom-vi-darwin` (the ex/vi lineage).  See
also that repo's `PROVENANCE.md`.

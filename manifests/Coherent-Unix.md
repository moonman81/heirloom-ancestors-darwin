# Coherent (Mark Williams Company, 1983-1994)

Mark Williams Company's commercial V7 clone — the first Unix-like
system for IBM PC / DOS-compatible hardware. Later released as
open-source. 220 MB on the mirror covering base, compiler,
DemonArchives, documents, DOS bootstraps, ghostscript.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Other/Coherent/`

## Content

`base/`, `compiler/`, `documents/`, plus DOS-hosted DemonArchives.

## Why this matters to Heirloom

An independent V7-lineage implementation. Not directly ancestral to
Heirloom but illustrates the alternative-Unix ecosystem V7 spawned.

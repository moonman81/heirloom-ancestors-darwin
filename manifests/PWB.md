# PWB/UNIX — Programmer's Workbench (Bell Labs, ~1977)

The intermediate stage between V7 and System III.  The direct
progenitor of System V and, transitively, of every SVR4/OpenSolaris
utility heirloom-toolchest-darwin ports.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/USDL/bostic_pwb.tar.gz`
  (Keith Bostic's tape)
- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/USDL/spencer_pwb.tar.gz`
  (Henry Spencer's tape)

Two independent donor tapes for cross-verification.

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Explains how AT&T commands evolved from V7 (Research) to System V
(Commercial).  The Ritter Heirloom lineage runs through this stage.

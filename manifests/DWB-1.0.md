# Documenter's Workbench 1.0 (AT&T, 1988)

The canonical AT&T Documenter's Workbench that
heirloom-doctools-darwin's `src/` descends from (via OpenSolaris).
DWB 1.0 is the earlier direct ancestor.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Documenters_Workbench/dwb_1.0.tar.gz`
  (971 KB)

## Content summary

DWB 1.0 contains:
- Original troff, nroff, dpost (device PostScript)
- tbl, eqn, pic, grap preprocessors (the same pipeline
  heirloom-doctools-darwin ships)
- mm, ms macros
- ndx, refer bibliographic tools

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Enables source-diff study of what heirloom-doctools-darwin's port
preserves vs modernises against DWB 1.0.

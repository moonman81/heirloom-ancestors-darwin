# 1BSD through 4BSD (UC Berkeley, 1977-1986)

Berkeley's early additions before the AT&T/BSD split became large.
The ucb/ personality variants in heirloom-toolchest-darwin trace
their initial shape to these releases.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UCB/1BSD/1bsd.tar.gz` (~1977-78)
- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UCB/2BSD/2bsd.tar.gz` (~1978)
- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UCB/3BSD/3bsd.tar.gz` (~1979)
- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UCB/4BSD/` (~1980)

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Companion to the 32V manifest.  32V is what AT&T shipped; the
early BSDs are what Bill Joy et al. added on top.  Explains the
tools and semantics that end up in heirloom-toolchest-darwin's
ucb/ directory.

## Licensing

BSD-style with residual AT&T dependencies in some tools.
See `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/UCB/README.html`.

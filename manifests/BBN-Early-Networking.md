# BBN early Unix networking (Bolt Beranek Newman, 1976-1980)

BBN's TCP/IP + early network subsystem additions to Unix — the
codebase that seeded 4.2BSD's networking stack and the first
Internet-Unix bindings.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Early_Networking/BBN/jnc_bbn.tar.gz`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Historically pivotal but licensing status is unclear (BBN
contracts + AT&T V6-lineage overlay). Manifest only; no bundling.

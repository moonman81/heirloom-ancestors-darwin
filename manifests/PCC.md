# Portable C Compiler (S. C. Johnson, AT&T, ~1978)

The AT&T Portable C Compiler — the compiler-suite ancestor of
heirloom-devtools-darwin's ccs/.  Four architecture ports preserved
on the TUHS Archive.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Portable_CC/`
- Individual arch ports at:
  `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Portable_CC/16032.zip`
  `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Portable_CC/286.zip`
  `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Portable_CC/68000.zip`
  `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Applications/Portable_CC/8086.zip`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

If heirloom-devtools-darwin work ever moves toward historical
faithfulness for the ccs compiler suite, PCC is the reference.

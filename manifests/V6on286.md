# V6-on-286 (early 1980s)

A port of V6 Unix to the Intel 80286. Historically important as
the technical bridge between V6 and Xenix's commercial x86 arrival.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Other/V6on286/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Documents the V6 → x86 transition point. Adjacent to Coherent and
Xenix in the alt-Unix constellation.

# Xinu — Xinu Is Not Unix (Douglas Comer, Purdue, 1980s)

Doug Comer's teaching operating system. Small, well-documented,
inspired by V6 Unix. Used pedagogically at Purdue and many other
universities for OS courses.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Other/Xinu/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Teaching Unix lineage. Complements MERT and V6-Vax as compatibility
/ derivative examples.

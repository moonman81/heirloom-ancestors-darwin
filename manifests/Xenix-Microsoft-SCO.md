# Xenix (Microsoft → SCO, 1980-1989)

Microsoft's V7-based Unix for x86, later maintained and expanded
by SCO. Historically important as the first commercially widespread
Unix on personal computers.

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Distributions/Other/Xenix/`

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Illustrates the AT&T-licensed V7 → commercial-x86 branch.
Not ancestral to Heirloom's SVR4 line but historically parallel.

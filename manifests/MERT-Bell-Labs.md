# MERT — Multi-Environment Real-Time (Bell Labs, ~1979-1981)

Bell Labs' realtime port of V6/V7 Unix. A separate branch that
influenced SVR2's realtime and streams work. Preserved on TUHS
primarily as a manual-page corpus (Release 0, 509 PDFs, 375 MB).

## Location on TUHS Archive

- `https://www.tuhs.org/Archive/Documentation/Manuals/MERT_Release_0/`
  (documentation)

## Content

The MERT Release 0 manual is a 509-PDF corpus — one of the most
extensive AT&T internal manual collections preserved.

## Why this matters to Heirloom

Realtime + streams heritage that flowed into SVR2/SVR3. Not
directly ancestral to any heirloom-*-darwin package but
bibliographically significant.
